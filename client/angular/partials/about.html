The goal of Rush Reads is to empower readers to digest material more quickly and efficiently. We don't think that all texts should be read in the format of our excercises, but we do beleive that practicing under certain conditions can help our users become faster and more effective readers.

​

1. First
We recommend that readers take a diagnositc to find out how fast they read now.

​

2. Practice
Practice your speed reading skills by trying one of our two speed reading excercises.

​

3. Re-Assess
After you've tried a few of our excercises, try taking a second diganostic exam and see if you've become a faster reader!
