The Difference is in the Research

As the University of Utah School of Medicine reports, the average person reads around 300 words per minute. Speed readers can read between 500 and 700 words per minute -- or more. As long as your comprehension of reading material remains stable or increases, speed reading can greatly improve your efficiency.
